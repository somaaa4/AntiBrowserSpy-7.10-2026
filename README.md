## 📖 About

AntiBrowserSpy 7.10 2026 is a standalone Windows utility designed to help you protect your privacy while browsing the web. It blocks tracking scripts, removes spyware cookies, and prevents websites from fingerprinting your browser. This tool gives you granular control over what data your browser shares, reducing unwanted surveillance and targeted advertising without breaking site functionality.
